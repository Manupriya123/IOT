ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Cubical Laboratories Pvt. Ltd was incorporated in November 2013, by the bright minds of IIT Guwahati, India. We are a Delhi based company with the head office located in Dwarka, New Delhi.

Over the past two years, we have established a wide network of distributors and dealers in 17 states (70 cities ) in India, and till the end of this year, we aim to expand to more states along with a few south east Asian and Middle Eastern countries.

The primary vision of our founders is to provide high quality, reliable and affordable solution to control or monitor the electrical appliances remotely with minimal human effort in the Indian market. Along with households, we are currently focusing on hotels , office spaces and major housing developers as well.

In the next 5 years ,we see ourselves being present in majority of Indian homes , owing to the ease of use of our products, our commendable easy installation, after sales services and the fact that our products are 2-3 times more economically viable than those currently present in the market.

With our patented communication protocol, Cube-R which is more suitable for the Indian market because of less data consumption and it’s ability to integrate with other protocols, we wish to bring a revolution in the industry of IoT ; as we see the future of our company intimately connected to it.

WHO WE ARE

Who we are as an organization is a direct result of the kind of people we have employed and the atmosphere we have created in our workplace , which in turn reflects the core values , culture, the mission and vision of our company.

With a staff strength of 100+ inspired employees, we are a team of highly motivated IOT enthusiasts with the mission of building the ultimate, comprehensive and affordable home automation solution from India.

WHAT WE DO

We are an IoT company which develops, designs, manufactures and markets our product. Our products include home automation products along with a few peripheral ones for households, offices , hotels etc. Technically , we give you intelligent homes and smarter living by creating software, fabricating hardware and combing through terabytes of data.

Broadly , the services we provide are –

Home Automation installation and After sales services(e.g. guaranteed timely replacement ) Research and development is one of our major focus , with the aim of developing complex technology which is easy to use and maintain. The basis of our business formation was recognizing a need in the market and putting in efforts to fulfill it. And we keep this philosophy as the background of everything that we do, which leads to constant evolution of what we do and the way we do it ; to deliver what our customers need and want.

We have put in years of research and development and continue to do so to generate complex yet simple, high quality and reliable technology.
